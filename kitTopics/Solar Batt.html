SOLAR BATTERY CHARGER
( kit code-KP 3015)

This solar charger, charges a 12V battery from solar panels. An electronic control circuit sets automatic cut-off when the battery is fully charged .
There is also a feedback automatic voltage regulator (AVR) to control charging at different intensity of sunshine.

Battery Capacity : 60aH
Battery Voltage : 12V
Charging Current : 5A
This kit is not sold with the solar panel. A solar panel of minimun 60W would be required.
