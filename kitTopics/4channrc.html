4 CHANNEL WIRELESS REMOTE CONTROL
( kit code-KF 4011)

This 4 channel remote control uses a 434MHz transciever module, with a range of approximately 40m ( which will suffice for indoor use). Flip-flop and phase locked loop circuits are used to discriminate the signals for switching of different relays to different loads.
Transmitter frequency : 434MHz
Supply Voltage : 12Vdc
Load Voltage : 220Vac
Load Current : 5A (max.)
Range : 40Meters (approx).
