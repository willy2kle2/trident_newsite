HIGH POWER FM TRANSMITTER (15WATTS)
( kit code-KF 4005)

When talking about RF power, 15Watts is monster!! you can build a complete FM broadcast station to cover the whole city of Lagos with a 15Watt Transmitter. In fact "RAY POWER 100 FM" transmitts with just a little over 10W and covers most of the western states. This kit delivers 15Watts of RF power to the antenna. The circuit uses four transistor based amplifier stages and a final preamplifier stage. Modulation is achieved via a regenrative feedback transistor (colpitts oscillator) stage.
The input has a preamplifier stage and accepts any line level audio source e.g a CD or Tape OUT, mixing deck, computer sound card, MP3 player etc. Also, the input gain is adjustable.
Frequency Range : 88 - 108MHz
Supply Voltage : 14V (3.5A)
Range : 100KM radius (approx.)
