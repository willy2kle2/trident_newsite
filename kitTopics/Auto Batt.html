AUTOMATIC BATTERY CHARGER
( kit code-KP 3013)

This battery charger combines comparator, flip-flops and thyristor control circuits to execute automatic control of battery charging. The circuit starts charging when the battery is discharged and automatically stops charging when the battery is fully charged.
Battery Capacity : 60Ah - 200Ah
Charging Voltage : 12V / 24V (using a select switch)
Charging Current : 5A (typical)
