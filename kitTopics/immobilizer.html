CAR IMMOBILIZER
( kit code-KA 1005)

This project uses timimg circuits and a thyristor to disable the ignition of a car after the car might have been stolen and driven away. Exit/entry delay circuit helps the user to disable the unit, so the car can tell when it is stolen or when an unauthorised user is trying to drive it.
