TELEPHONE TRANSMITTER (phone bug)
( kit code-KF 4027)

This transmitter starts transmitting as soon as the handset is lifted. It is powered from the phone line, and the phone line serves as the aerial. It would transmit both conversation to an FM reciever.
Range : 200meters (approx).
Frequency : (88 -108MHz)
Supply Voltage : 9V
