WIRELESS TRANSCIEVER
( kit code-KP 4023)

This is a 434MHz wireless transciever incorporating a wireless remote control used to turn (ON/OFF) a reading lamp and could be used to control security lights, alarm circuits, and generally any AC or DC powered equipment.
Frequency : 434MHz
Range : 40Meters (approx.)
Supply Voltage : 9V
