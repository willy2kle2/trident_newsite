Digital Frequency Counter
( kit code-KF 4003)

This is a 3digit frequncy counter and can measure up to 1KHz at a resolution of 1Hz, and 999KHz at a resolution of 1KHz. The input is designed to take both Sine, Square and triangle waveforms. Output is displayed on a 7-segment display.
Supply Voltage: 5Vdc
Current (Max): 200mA
Frequency Range:0-1MHz (999KHz).
