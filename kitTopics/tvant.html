TV ANTENNA BOOSTER
( kit code-KF 4015)

This is a high gain , broadband aerial amplifier (signal booster), suitable for improving picture quality of TV (VHF and UHF) broadcast, including digital TV, in areas where poor signal reception is experienced.
Frequency Range : 40 - 860MHz
Signal Gain : 15 - 22dB
Input/output impedance : 75 Ohm
Supply Voltage : 12Vdc
