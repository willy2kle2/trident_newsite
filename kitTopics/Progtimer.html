PROGRAMMABLE TIMER
( kit code-KF 4025)

Many electronic applications require some form of timing or the other, for instance you need to be able to time how long your food would stay inside a microwave oven to prevent it from getting burnt or over cooked.
This programmable timer would control a relay (which could switch ON/OFF) power to an equipment, when the programmed time elapses. The circuit uses astable oscillator and a 12 stage counter .
