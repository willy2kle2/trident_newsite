HEAVY DUTY BATTERY CHARGER
( kit code-KP 3019)

This battery charger is designed to charge a bank of batteries. charging current is adjustable (with max. charge current of 20A). The circuit uses a switching power supply for the generation of the charge voltage, and control circuits ( including op-amps, flip-flops and thyristors ) for automatic control of the charging.


Battery Capacity : 800aH (max. for battery bank)
Charging Voltage : 12V - 48V (adjustable)
Charging Current : 20A (max)
