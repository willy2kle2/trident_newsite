TOUCH SENSITIVE SWITCH
( kit code-KM 4025)

This switch activates an alarm when a person or object touches a fixed contact point. The circuit uses capacitive loading principle.
Supply voltage : 12V
Current(Max): 250mA
Alarm type: Two-Tone siren Module.
