FM RECIEVER
( kit code-KF 4009)

This kit is a "superhet" fm reciever. The circuit employs a single chip demodulator combined with a varicap tuning to provied high stability and a TDA2003 amplifier.
Frequency Range : 88 - 108MHz.
Operating Voltage : 9 - 12Vdc
Supply Current : 500mA
S/N ratio : 60dB
Distortion : 0.7%
