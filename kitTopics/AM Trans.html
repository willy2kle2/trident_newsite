AM TRANSMITTER
( kit code-KF 4001)

This kit uses transistor stages to provide amplitude modulation of voice over the medium wave band. The circuit is composed of a pre-amplifier stage, RF oscillator, AM modulator, and a class C amplifier stage.
Frequency Range : 550 - 1600KHz.
Supply Voltage : 9V
Power : 0.5W
Range : 200meters (approx).
