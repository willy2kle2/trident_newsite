OPTICAL FIBRE COMMUNICATIONS LINK
( kit code-KM 2515)

This kit allows audio communications via fibre optic cables provided. Fibre optic cables are immune to interference from electrically noisy environment (in addition to other benefits). The circuit uses a matched transmitter & reciever pair. The transmitter oard uses a microphone and IC to modulate a LED. The reciever board demodulates the light signal and drives the speaker provided. 4Meters of optical fibre cable is provided but the baord could drive up to 150meters.
Supply Voltage:12V , Current (Max.): 500mA
