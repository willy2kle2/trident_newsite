SOLAR INVERTER
( kit code-KP 3007)

This kit is a 12V inverter featuring a 60W solar battery charger. The system has automatic control for solar charging (as well as charging from the mains) and overload shutdown for the inverter stage.
Output Voltage : 220 - 240VAC
Power : 500VA
Battery Voltage : 12Vdv
Solar Panel : 60Watts (not supplied)
Frequency : 50Hz
