MICROWAVE OVEN
( kit code-KP 3023)

Microwaves are a form of electromagnetic radiation. They will pass through glass, paper, and most plastics but they are reflected by metals and are absorbed by water and many other components of food.
This microwave oven kit is composed of a magnetron (which generates the microwave),touch pad electronic timing and control circuits (to control the generation of the microwaves and time the cooking period). The electronic control stage employed use PIC16F877 microcontroller.
