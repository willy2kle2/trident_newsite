PIC ALARM CLOCK
(kit code-PIC 120)
PROJECTS HOME PAGE

This Project is a Micro-Controller Based Digital Alarm Clock. The Design uses a PIC16F84 Micro-Controller, and displays both the hours and minutes of the clock. Three input buttons are provided to enable the setting and resetting of the clock.
