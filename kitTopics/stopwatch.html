Digital Stop Watch
(kit code-KM 2530)

The digital stopwatch can time events for duration of approximately 90s. The system is hand held and once triggered will start counting in seconds till a push button switch is used to stop the counting.The last count is Latched till the system is reset for another timing sequence to start. Applications include time events like debates & timing of events in the laboratory (like the no of oscillations of the swinging of a pendulum for a given time). Components used include timers, oscillators, counters, and other logic ICs
